How to Play Word Weavers

Welcome to Word Weavers! Here's how to get started:

  • For a **Regular Game**, you will start with 16 random letter tiles drawn from a large pool of over 100 letters. For the **Daily Puzzle**, everyone receives the same specific set of 16 letters for that day.
  • Drag and drop letters onto the 12x12 grid to form words.
  • Words must be formed crossword-style (letters must connect to form words horizontally or vertically).
  • All letters provided must be used on the board to complete the puzzle.
  • Drawing Tiles (Regular Game): During a regular game, you can click the "Draw Tile" button to get an additional random tile from the pool to add to your hand.
  • Click the "Done" button when you've used all your letters and believe your words are correct according to standard English.
  • Letter Exchange: You can exchange letter tiles from your hand. Click "Exchange Letters", select tiles, then "Confirm Exchange".
    • In a **Regular Game**, exchanged letters are discarded from play. You'll receive three new random letters from the main pool for each one exchanged.
    • In the **Daily Puzzle**, exchanges also provide three new letters, drawn from its unique daily set of available letters.
    A time penalty of 15 seconds per tile exchanged is added to your total time in both modes.
  • A timer will count up to track how long you take. Your score will be based on word length, letter difficulty, and a bonus for completing the puzzle quickly (the bonus decreases over the first 3 minutes).

Good luck and have fun weaving words!
